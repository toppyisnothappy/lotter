# Product Guidelines - Lotter

This document defines the principles and standards for the Lotter user experience.

## Voice and Tone
**Friendly and Approachable**: The product should feel easy to use and helpful, aimed at minimizing the inherent stress of retail environments.

## Design Principles
1. **Simplicity over Features**: Prioritize clear, intuitive workflows over complex feature sets.
2. **High Performance First**: Every interaction, especially in the POS loop, must be optimized for speed.

## Visual Standards
While a "Premium Experience" is a project goal, we prioritize **simplicity and functionality**. Visual elements like dark mode and animations should enhance speed and clarity rather than distracting from the task at hand.
