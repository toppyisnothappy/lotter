# Product Definition - Lotter

A modern retail management platform for inventory, credit sales, and multi-tenant store operations.

## Problem Statement
Retailers lack integrated tools to manage both high-speed POS sales and complex, long-term customer installment debts.

## Target Users
Retail Store Owners & Clerks: Owners managing inventory and debt, and clerks handling high-speed daily sales.

## Key Goals
1. **High-Performance POS**: Achieving sub-second item search and add-to-cart functionality.
2. **Debt Integrity**: Maintaining 100% accurate, immutable audit logs for customer installment payments.
3. **Premium Experience**: Delivering a "wow factor" UI with dark mode, glassmorphism, and smooth animations.
