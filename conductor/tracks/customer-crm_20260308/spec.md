# Specification: Customer Debt Management (CRM)

**Track ID:** customer-crm_20260308
**Type:** Feature
**Created:** 2026-03-08
**Status:** Draft

## Summary

Implementing a comprehensive Customer Relationship Management (CRM) interface to handle customer profiles, track long-term installment debts, and view immutable transaction audits.

## Context

Lotter differentiates itself by allowing store owners to manage complex off-the-books installment debts. A dedicated dashboard is required to search customers, see their current debt balance, log new payments toward that debt, and review their transaction history.

## User Story

As a Store Owner, I want to manage customer profiles and track their open credit installments so that I can accurately collect debt and provide them with a full audit log of their purchases.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Store owners can register new customer profiles and search existing ones.
- [ ] Each customer profile displays a real-time total of their outstanding debt balance.
- [ ] Store owners can process "Partial Payments" that reduce a specific customer's debt balance.
- [ ] Customers have a detailed, immutable timeline (audit log) showing every POS transaction and debt payment they have made.
- [ ] All operations are securely isolated by the active `organization_id`.

## Dependencies

- **backend-migration_20260305**: Depends heavily on the `customers`, `debts`, and `transactions` tables migrated to MySQL.
- **pos-terminal_20260308**: Must be cleanly separated from the POS cart flow, though POS transactions may link to customers.

## Out of Scope

- Automated SMS or Email reminders for debt collection.
- Credit scoring algorithms.
- Complex interest calculations (debts are flat totals for now).

## Technical Notes

- Keep the CRM interface separated from the POS clerk interface.
- Debt Integrity is critical. A payment MUST result in an atomic database transaction that logs the payment and decrements the debt balance simultaneously to prevent race conditions.
